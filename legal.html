The short version

  • Your DNA and biometric files never leave your device. We never receive, see, upload, or store your raw data.
  • For orders, we only handle what's needed to ship a print and send updates: your name, address, and email (via Stripe).
  • We don't run ad trackers or sell your data.

Privacy

Your biological data stays on your device

When you upload a file to create your artwork, it is read and processed entirely in your browser. Your raw genetic or health data is never sent to us or anyone else, never uploaded, and never stored. The only thing produced is the finished image.

What we do collect

To make and ship a print, our payment processor (Stripe) collects your name, shipping address, and email at checkout. We use these only to fulfill and deliver your order and to send order/shipping updates. Your generated artwork (the image — not your data) is uploaded to image hosting so it can be printed and minted.

Who we work with

Stripe (payments), Cloudinary (image hosting), Prodigi (printing & shipping), and thirdweb / the Base blockchain (NFT minting). Each processes data under its own policy.

Cookies & analytics

The site is static and does not set tracking cookies.

NFTs are public and permanent

Blockchains are public and permanent. Your artwork and non-identifying details (a one-way seed code and the date) are published on-chain — never your raw data. Once minted, on-chain records cannot be deleted.

Your choices

Email genepiart@gmail.com to ask what order information we hold or to request deletion of what we can remove. (On-chain records and completed-order records held by Stripe/Prodigi may persist.)
